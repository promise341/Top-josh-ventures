Our Story

Nigeria's Finest
Farm Produce
Dealers

Top Josh Ventures is a reputable agro-commodity trading company headquartered in Ikom, Cross River State. We specialise in the procurement, processing, and trading of premium farm produce — connecting local farmers to regional and international buyers.

With deep roots in the Cross River agricultural ecosystem, our expert team ensures every shipment meets the highest standards of quality and traceability. From rich cocoa pods to crisp cashew seeds and nutrient-dense kernel, we source the best nature has to offer.

Chief Josh
Founder & Principal Merchant — Top Josh Ventures
